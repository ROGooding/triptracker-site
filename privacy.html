TripTracker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: TripTracker uses GPS to calculate routes, distances, and fares.
  • Trip Details: We store trip summaries (time, distance, fare) for your convenience.
  • Email (Optional): If you choose to send invoices via email, TripTracker will use the address you provide.

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, phone number, or contacts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate and display trip fares.
  • To generate trip history and summaries.
  • To send invoices to your email (if requested).
  • To improve app performance and reliability.

We do not sell, share, or trade your data with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Trip data is stored locally on your device. Email invoices are sent securely using your chosen email client. We implement safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

TripTracker does not currently integrate with advertisers or analytics providers. If advertising is added in future versions, this policy will be updated to reflect how Advertising ID and related data are handled.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: triptrackercustomerservice@gmail.com

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and reflected in the app.
